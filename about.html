About Us

Hi, I’m Nikhil Kanojia — a travel vlogger with a deep love for animals. During my travels, I kept encountering stray animals walking dangerously close to roads, invisible to drivers, and at risk. That’s when I decided to do something meaningful with my journey.

I’ve launched a solo initiative to donate reflective safety collars to every stray animal I come across while traveling — be it a dog, cow, or cat. These collars help increase visibility at night and prevent tragic accidents.

My mission is to reach 1,000 animals this year, and I’m making that happen on each trip I take.

Even if you can't donate, you can still support me. Head over to my YouTube channel to see how I carry out this initiative. If it inspires you, please subscribe and share — your support matters.

Let’s bring love, safety, and compassion to every stray we cross paths with. Together, we can create real change — one collar, one life, one journey at a time.
